About us

About FELLA

Welcome to FELLA, where creativity comes alive.

Nestled in the heart of the city, FELLA is more than just a stationery shop—it's a sanctuary for artists, writers, and dreamers. With a passion for the finer details and a commitment to quality, FELLA offers an inspiring array of products that transform everyday moments into extraordinary experiences.

Our Story

FELLA was founded by Felix Lane, a dedicated artist with a vision. Felix believed that the tools we use to create should be as inspiring as the work itself. His dream was to build a place where people could find not only the finest stationery but also a sense of community and inspiration. This vision came to life with FELLA, named after Felix Lane's initials and symbolizing the friendly, welcoming spirit of our shop.

Our Community

FELLA is more than a store—it's a hub for creativity. We regularly host workshops and events that bring people together to learn, create, and share. Whether you're attending a calligraphy class, participating in a watercolor workshop, or joining a storytelling evening, you'll find a supportive and inspiring community at FELLA.

Our Promise

At FELLA, we believe in the power of creativity to transform lives. Our mission is to provide you with the tools and inspiration to express yourself and bring your ideas to life. We are committed to offering high-quality products and exceptional service, ensuring that every visit to FELLA is a delightful and inspiring experience.



Join us at FELLA and discover a world where creativity knows no bounds. Whether you're a seasoned artist or just beginning your creative journey, we invite you to explore, create, and be inspired.
